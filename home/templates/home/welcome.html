How to Play

Make Picks

Each week, select who you think will win each game. Each pick you guess correctly earns you 25 points. You always want to make your picks because this a free way to earn points without any risk of losing them. Picks will lock 1 minute before the game starts.

Place Bets

This is where you can use your hard earned points from correct picks to bet on the spread of the game. If you're not sure how to bet on the spread of the game, click the link below. Bet as much or as little as you would like, and keep in mind you do not need to bet on every game. The payout is equal to 1.9X your bet (Multiply bet amount by 1.9). If your bet is incorrect though, you lose the points you put down. Bets will lock 1 minute before the game starts.

Compete in Your League

Throughout the season you will be competing with your league to see who can score the most amount of points. The winner of each league will get special account accolades which you can flaunt the following season!

Good luck!
